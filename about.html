AVON Cinemas Test

AVON Cinemas is a local Rhode Island theater in Providence, Rhode Island. We offer cheap, high-quality movie theaters & showings, a delicious snack bar, and a convenient location!

AVON Cinemas was started in 1938 on Providence's East Art Deco side. Our theater and is available for location shootings, screenings, and premieres.

For more information, contact Kenneth R. Dulgarian at (401) 421-0020.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

We are located on Thayer Street, in Providence, Rhode Island.

What are your prices?

$11.00 For Adults, $8.50 for Matinees, $8.50 ALWAYS for Children.

Are there special passes, or gift cards?

Absolutely! Six-Show passes are available for $51.00, and Gift Cards are good for one regular-priced ($11.00) admission!
