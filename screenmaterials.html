Screenprinting Materials

Necessary Screenprinting Materials

(~$60-$70 Start-Up)

  1. Frame (Speedball is used, 100-150 Mesh)
  2. Emulsion + Activator/Sensitizer (Speedball Diazo Kit is used)
  3. Squeegee (Rounded is better than pointed)
  4. Screenprinting Ink (Different color than the shirt you are using)
  5. Large Plastic, Glass, or Plexiglass Sheet (The heavier the better)
  6. Clear Plastic Printer Paper (Standard Size)

Other Stuff

(necessary household stuff)

  1. Sunlight/Bright Light & Darkness
  2. Hose, Showerhead, or Faucet Sprayer (cold water!!)
  3. Printing Surface
  4. Cardboard or Other Block (Ink will get on it and will not easily wash out/off)
  5. Gloves, or really good handwashing skills
  6. Tape (Duct or Masking, but never Duck)

Other (Optional) Stuff

(i did use some of these, but you absolutely do not need any of them to screenprint)

  1. Black Paper/Black Surface
  2. Scrub Brush
  3. Screen Coater (basically a large fancy scoop)
  4. A screenprinting press
  5. A heat press
  6. A screenprinting lightbox
