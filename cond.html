Screenprinting Tutorial!

Be sure to check out the materials page, first!

  1. Design something!
  2. Print two copies.
  3. Clean & dry the screen.
  4. Dissolve sensitizer.
  5. Activate emulsion.
  6. Coat one side of the screen in emulsion.
  7. Coat other side of screen in emulsion.
  8. Allow time to dry (horizantally, overnight).
  9. Burn images backwards onto the screen, use bright light, then pitch darkness.
  10. Rinse extra emulsion off, allow time to dry.
  11. Wash & dry T-Shirt/Other without dryer sheets or fabric softener.
  12. Slide a piece of cardboard or other block inside the shirt.
  13. Tape off frame (edges and missed spots).
  14. Lay frame flat on flattened shirt.
  15. Run ink down the shirt once (softly), then again (harder). Repeat a few times if using black ink.
  16. Heat dry shirt, and enjoy!
