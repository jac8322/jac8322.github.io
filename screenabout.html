What is Screenprinting?

Screenprinting is the method of printing, off of a screen (hence the name...). You can print any design, logo, or piece of art onto a shirt, canvas, pants, desk (although a hard surface won't work too well), or really anything you want! Essentially, you print the design onto clear paper (if you draw it; scan it and edit out the unused paper, then reprint it onto transparency), burn it onto a screen, then print it with ink & a squeegee onto whatever surface you wish!

The first time you ever screenprint, there is a bit of a start-up fee (between ~$60-~$70 depending on where you buy the materials and which brands you buy), but it gets considerably cheaper after the first purchase (depending on if you reclaim the frame, use the same design, and how many shirts you wish to print, it could be anywhere from ~$3-~$70 for each design and/or shirt after that).

Screenprinting is a great, fun, and (relatively) cheap way to advertise your designs, make unique clothing, and even make a bit of extra money! It's easy to do, and quick to learn!
