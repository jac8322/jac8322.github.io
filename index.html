ABOUT US

We are an Australian-inspired coffee and pastry shop located in Providence, Rhode Island, near PPAC. We pride ourselves on serving high-quality, authentic, delicious, and affordable cofffee and treats. We also serve various teas. All of our products are organically sourced, and we will happily answer any questions as to where our products have come from and how they were raised.

COFFEE

We serve a large variety of coffee and espresso beverages. All expertly made, organically sourced, and served. Flat whites, lattes, cappucinos, macchiatos, iced and hot coffee, straight espresso, americanos, and more.

TEAS

We offer many unique and interesting teas. Both iced and hot - fresh brewed for each person. Mix and match teas and ingredients to create a unique and personal tea every time.

BAKED GOODS

While we do not serve sandwiches or meals, we have a selection of delicious treats and baked good to pair with your beverage.
